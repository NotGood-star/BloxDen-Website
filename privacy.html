Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BloxDen collects only the data necessary to provide its services effectively. This includes:

  • Discord User IDs and Guild (Server) IDs.
  • Configuration settings you save within the bot.
  • Roblox usernames (if linked) for verification purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data strictly to ensure the bot functions correctly. This includes processing commands, maintaining moderation logs, and managing server settings. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices on trusted hosting platforms. We strive to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. If you wish to have your server or personal data removed from our database, please reach out to us via our support server, and we will process your request promptly.

If you have any questions regarding your privacy, please join our Support Server.
